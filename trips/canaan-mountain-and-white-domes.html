Adventures - Canaan Mountain and White Domes

12 /29/2 4 - Canaan Mountain wilderness and White Domes

Driving through the town of Hildale or Colorado City, Im not really sure which one as they are right next to eachother, there is the Canaan Mountain Wilderness. I had long been interested in exploring the area but never go around to it. The temperature today was above normal, in the 50s in late December in the high desert. Not bad. The trail started up a hill then climbed through a small canyon, then got difficult to find as it switchbacked the side of a mountain. Almost to the top the trail seemed to end at a 6 foot slab of vertical stone. I kept looking for where to go then finally realized the wall ahd to be scaled. There werent many footholds or things to grab onto but i finally located a nice piece of rock to hold on and pull myself over. From there the trail just about disappeared and i wandered through ponderosa while admiring the view out across the wilderness. I found my way down to a dry sandy wash with ice in spots, the ascended towards white domes. I could see white domes from a ways a way but when i got there i was totally blown away. It was similar to yant flat but white and on top of a mountain saddle overlooking zion in the far distance. The waves of rock were incredible and had a gentle drift to it. I took a bunch of photos then looped around and took the squirrel canyon trail back to the car. I hightailed it home and got back just in time to watch the 'Skins beat the falcons in an overtime, second to last game of the season, must-win game.
