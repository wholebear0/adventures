North Ajo Peak

2025-12-27 - North Ajo Peak

I failed to make it to the top of this one and got to a point just shy of the saddle below the summit block. Perhaps a lack of mental preparation having decided to attempt it a few hours earlier, or the short steep slopes with loose scree and both ankles still not completely healed, or likely, a combination of both. North Ajo Peak is a very small standalone almost-hill mountain surrounded by larger mountains and has a steep slope. I parked a mile and a half away and walked in the rough roads then once near the peak, bushwhacked over small hills and up a gulley. As it got steeper, the scree got noticeably looser and footing became challenging. As I climbed upwards the rock quality continued to deteriorate and wedged along a slope I called it a day. This way one that got away. I walked the mile and a half back to the car in a setting sun with head dropped low in disappointment.
