Mt Nebo

7/12/2025 - Mt Nebo

This was a fun 9 mile hike to the top of 11,928’ Mt Nebo and was easy to get to just off I-15. The area surrounding these mountains is a wilderness area with a scenic byway and many places for high elevation dispersed camping along the way. Midway up the meandering road at about 9,000 ft is the trailhead to Mount Nebo. It is a popular trail and being a Saturday the parking lot was full though the hike itself didn’t feel too overcrowded. At the start of the hike there is a gain in elevation which continues pretty much to the peak.  Along the way there is a pass called Wolf pass and above it a tall false summit with Mt Nebo hiding above and past it. Once atop the false summit the trail continues along a nice ridgeline that looks steeper and more dangerous than it actually is. There are great views in all directions, west towards the town of Nephi, east towards the forest, and north towards Salt Lake City and the Wasatch mountains.
