Mt Kino Approach

2025-12-28 - Mt Kino Approach

This was a well off the beaten path hike in the Organ Pipe Cactus Wilderness requiring a 16 mile drive along washboarded, lightly traveled gravel road. My original plans were to head home on this day but I decided to hang around one more day to do this hike. After the hour long drive to the trailhead I realized I was running low on food supplies for the hike so I planned to turn around once half of the food was gone. Unfortunately, the lack of food got the best of me and I had to turn around somewhat near the summit, near an interesting land bridge connecting a ridge to Mt Kino. Most of the hike, about 5 miles, was flat and through a wash with some but not much discarded litter here and there from smuggling/migrants. I hope to return here in the future and make it to the top. The solitude and views were great.
