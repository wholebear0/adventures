Colter Bay to Little Mackinaw Island

8/23/24 - Colter Bay to Little Mackinaw Island

One of the last backcountry camping permits for that day wound up in my hands. Little Mackinaw island was only 3 miles away from the kayak launch at Colter Bay. The weather setting out looked questionable with dark grey rain clouds to the west looming over the mountains. I made it to the island fine and quickly set up camp before any rain would fall. I forgot to bring my hiking poles so I had to grab fallen tree branches and prop up the tent as best as possible. It held up despite looking like a poor man's hut. Not long after the sun gave way to storm clouds and there was some light rain. But in 30 minutes the sky cleared and there was a bright blue sky with sunshine. Later that evening the rain came back though just briefly. Never before had I seen weather change so quickly.
