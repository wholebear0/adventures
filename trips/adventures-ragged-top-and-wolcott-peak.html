Adventures - Ragged Top and Wolcott Peak

11/28/2025 - Ragged Top and Wolcott Peak

Ragged Top receives praise for being a nice off trail, day hike scramble and is about 18 miles west of I-10, though it left me slightly unfulfilled. I parked at the turn off to the road to the trailhead and was glad I did because the road was rough getting in. The road walk added about 2.5 miles round trip and wasn’t bad. I climbed around to the north side of the mountain while gaining some elevation then located the start of a gully system that rises to the saddle below the summit. Climbing up was relatively quick and painless except for a few thorns and cactus scratches. I'm not sure if i even climbed the right gulley as there seemed to be a wider and possibly easier way up to the west, on the other side of a rock wall. But there was a stray cairn or two which i followed like a lamb. However, the views were nice up to the saddle and beyond. Surrounding were neat rock formations. The climb up to the summit was also quick and up there was a pole with a solar panel and a summit register. I made my way back down to the saddle and turned south, descending a gully, then bushwhacked my way over to the base of Wolcott Mountain, which was another quick summit but offered a decent view of Ragged Top. The hike was okay but somewhat plain vanilla to me. It may be fun to explore some of the other pinnacles and rock formations near the summit sometime.
