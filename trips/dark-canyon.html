Adventures - Dark Canyon

9/27/2024 - Dark Canyon

I set out to do a longer, 1 night backpack trip, but I turned around after about 10 miles because of the complete lack of any water along the way and the hot sun. The trail was well maintained for the first 5 miles or so but quickly deteriorated into somewhat of a bushwhack and route find. I saw no one the whole day. The canyon seems like a pretty cool area though there wasn’t a whole lot that caught attention, aside from the two black bears I stumbled upon when I was nearly back at the car around dusk. Maybe I will visit the area again sometime to explore more.
