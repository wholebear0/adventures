Mt Tarumae

2026-06-21 - Mt Tarumae

I arrived at the trailhead early Sunday morning around 8 am and there were two parking attendants directing people where to park. The hike was straightforward with the trail ascending directly from the already crowded parking lot, up to the rim overlooking the Mt Tarumae volcano nearby. Hikers are not allowed near the volcano and there are signs and fencing just off the trail to discourage people from getting closer. The trail circled around the volcano so I stopped at two peaks along the way, Mt Nishiyama and an unnamed peak.
