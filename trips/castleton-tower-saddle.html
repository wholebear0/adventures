Castleton Tower Saddle

10/29/2021 - Castleton Tower Saddle

Dubbed as one of the more popular rock climbing landmarks in the country, Castleton tower is about 30 minutes or so outside of Moab. There is a trail leading up to the saddle which was nice and made even better by the golden leaves of cottonwood trees in the vicinity. I hiked up and saw a few people climbing the tower and got a nice panoramic view of the surrounding red rock canyonlands.
