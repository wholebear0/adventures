Adventures - Thimble Peak

1/11/2026 - Thimble Peak

It was a very cold morning, in the high 40s but the worst part was the wind. It was a constant 25-30 mph and near Thimble Peak gusted to probably over 40 mp h . I started from the Hirabayashi Campground and my fingers were stiff and cold by the time I made it to the base of Thimble Peak. There was some sno w in the distance at higher elevation and water flowing in the washes from recent rains. There was a 20 foot section of scrambling, probably high class 3, up a crack gulley in the rock. On top I realized there was a second summit, perhaps the real Thimble Peak just beyond but it looked like the same elevation. There was a drop which looked like I would have to descend and walk around to the bottom of the second peak to try getting up. There was some fixed rope but with the wind like it was I was content to call it a day.
