Burger Peak and Ridge via Forsyth Trail

6/1/2025 - Burger Peak and Ridge via Forsyth Trail

Bristlecone pines and their wild twisted roots along the ridge. Bristlecones are the oldest living tree in the world, living up to 5,000 years and can be found where nothing else grows in the high mountains of the Great Basin of Northern Nevada and Western Utah. Bristlecone Pines - Great Basin National Park (U.S. National Park Service)
