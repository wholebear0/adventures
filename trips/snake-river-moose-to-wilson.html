Adventures - Snake River, Moose to Wilson

8/24/24 - Snake River, Moose to Wilson

I unloaded the kayak and a few drybags from the car and parked the car in the boat launch parking lot. The arrangement was for a shuttle a.k.a some guy to go to the parking lot, pick up the keys which I stashed below the car next to the wheel, and drive it to a parking lot takeout miles down the river. Thankfully the shuttle went to plan and the Snake river was incredible. It followed parallel along the Teton mountains, meandering through shallow bends of crystal clear blue water. There were light rapids but never anything too treacherous.
