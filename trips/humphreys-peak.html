Humphreys Peak

6/28/2025 - Humphreys Peak

I slept overnight at the trail head parking lot and awoke at 6 am to a lot of commotion. The parking lot was already full and every minute more cars were arriving. Most of the hike, about a 4.5 mile hike up to Humphreys Peak, the tallest mountain in Arizona at 12,633' was a switchback route through pine forest. The views opened up at the top on the saddle, then the trail continued along the crest to the peak. There were nice views of the ski resort, Arizona Snowbowl, the mountain range inner basin, and surrounding flagstaff area.
