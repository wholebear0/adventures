Adventures - Butler Wash Ruins

4/7/2026 - Butler Wash Ruins

This route passed by two or three dwelling locations all on the west side of the wash. The most interesting one was the ballroom which requires a scramble up and over a 15 ft ledge. The ledge wasn't solid rock but packed sand so caution needs to be exercised. If I were a few inches shorter I don't think I would have made it. But on top at dwelling level was a big alcove with some dwellings. What was most notable was the depth of the alcove. It went back quite a ways like a cave, maybe one hundred feet.

Camping up on Elk Ridge near the Bears Ears that night
