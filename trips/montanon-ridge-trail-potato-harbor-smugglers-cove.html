Montanon Ridge Trail, Potato Harbor, Smugglers Cove

6/7/2025 - Montanon Ridge Trail, Potato Harbor, Smugglers Cove I woke up early and started hiking around 6:30 am in an effort to make the most of the day before the ferry back at 4 pm. I made a big loop, first heading up and north towards the ocean, then turning left and heading back to the ridge from yesterday, but continuing further along the ridge to Montanon Peak, then circling around and stopping at a beach before getting to the campground loop and ferry anchorage. The weather was about the same with fog, particularly along the ridge. It was a nice hike but I found the previous day’s one-way hike more enjoyable and scenic.  It was very possible that the weather was starting to dampen the mood. Two straight days of humid foggy weather in the 60s is definitely different than sunshine and dry weather pretty much every day.
