San Antonio Camino 1

10/18/2025 - San Antonio Camino 1

Today's Route, starting at the red marker and ending at the blue marker.

Starting bright and early at 8 am at Roosevelt Park by the old Lone Star Beer factory.

King William district

Southtown neighborhood

Near the convention center and tower of the Americas I cut through a parking lot and noticed a lady exiting her car. She noticed how the parking lot was surrounded by a fence and asked me for directions on how to leave the parking lot. I didnt really know so I pointed in a random direction. Later I found out there was a big national beer week festival a few blocks away. Maybe that's where she was headed.

Yanaguana Park

Vendors setting up for the National Beer Week festival. It didnt start for another couple hours but by the looks of it seemed really cool. There were hundreds of tents already set up, and in one area I spotted a line of about 40 portapotties.

Linking back up with the riverwalk near La Villita

One of the best sights in Texas...

Exiting the riverwalk, the Majestic Theater with man loitering in SUV outside.

Back on the riverwalk, heading north. There were quite a few joggers on a morning run.

It was at this spot 7 years ago around Christmas that we watched a nighttime Christmas float on the river

Pearl neighborhood. There was a farmers market of soets going on and there were tons of people so I didnt linger.

Continuing north through the St Mary's strip, a quiet and barren area during the day but wild at night. The venues here like their 80s, metal, and punk music, at least as my blurry memories recall.

After the St Mary's strip I found myself wandering through the Trinity University campus. There was a football game going on and open admission. I climbed up on the bleachers to rest a few minutes.

It was hot out, maybe in the low 90s so at Olmos Park I caught the 5 bus and the bus driver greeted me with "hello, how are you my love". I reached into my pocket to get a dollar but she said no need and to just go ahead. Good deal. The bus took me back to near the park from which I started, completing a nice day.
