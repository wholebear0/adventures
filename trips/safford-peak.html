Safford Peak

4/19/25 - Safford Peak

Today was an unusually mild day for April, in the 60s and breezy. For some time I had been wanting to hike up these small two peaks on the edge of town near Marana. Looking at maps I planned a route to hike up to Safford peak, then hike along the ridgeline for a half mile to a trail leading up to Panther Peak. But when I got up to the saddle just below Safford Peak I could not find a way across. The terrain was steep with loose scree rock, not to mention it just didn’t seem worth going to the second peak as the view would be nearly the same. Nevertheless, the hike up Safford Peak was a start to a day.
