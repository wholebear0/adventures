Adventures - White Rocks Trail to Cove Loop

5/31/2025 - White Rocks Trail to Cove Loop

A pleasant 14 mile hike through the mid ranges of the Pine Valley mountains. The temperature was nice, in the 60-70s while St George was in the 90 - 100s. There were numerous meadows and flats and the whole hike was surrounded by trees and grass and was quuite enjoyable. I only saw one other person, an old man who I passed that was wearing nearly identical clothing: long pants, long sleeve shirt, shade hat, two hiking poles. It was like passing by me in the future except the difference was that guy had a long beard.
