Adventures - Deertrap Mountain

9/17/2021 - Deertrap Mountain

After work on a late September Friday I headed over to Zion and picked up a backcountry permit for East Zion. It took an additional 30-40 minutes from the visitor center to the Stave Spring Trailhead where I parked the car. The hike to the rim was just a few miles and not the most scenic, but the view at the rim made up for it and then some. From the first photo below I turned off trail and headed south, unsure of what I would find. After a quarter of a mile navigating some small rocks I found a big peninsula of sorts jutting out from the mountain. On both sides there were 600-700+ ft drops but the 270 degree views were outstanding. Looking west were the East Temple and Moountain of the Sun, and looking south/southeast were numerous other peaks.
