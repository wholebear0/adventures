Adventures - Boquillas Canyon, Boquillas Mexico, and Hot Springs

4/17/2019 - Boquillas Canyon, Boquillas Mexico, and Hot Springs

3 different short excursions near the Boquillas crossing area in the far corner of Big Bend National Park, near the border: a short hike along the Rio Grande, a 1 minute boat road over the river and walk into the town of Boquillas, Mexico where we drank a cold beer, and the hot springs back on the US side.
