Mt Akaiwa and Mt Shimoakaiwa

06/22/2026 - Mt Akaiwa and Mt Shimoakaiwa

From Otaru I took a city bus from downtown which took about 15 minutes. After walking up a steep road to the trailhead, the trail began and the first half of the hike was through dense forest and had no views because of the vegetation even though it was right next to the coast. At My Shimoakaiwa the trees cleared a bit and there were some really nice views of the coast and some rocky cliffs. At one point I passed by what appeared to be climbers with helmets, then there were some steep cliffy crags that had some anchors drilled in. I continued down to the aquarium area where there was a small parking lot size amusement park, then walked along the road on a sidewalk to the hardware store where I purchased a small dolly to transport my suitcase whose wheels had become broken.
