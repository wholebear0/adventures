Adventures - Procession Panel

4/5/2026 - Procession Panel

The hike here was pretty quick and well marked with cairns and a social trail so the petroglyphs will be hard to miss. I found these unique in that they seemed to be telling a story of movement, something the artists were quite familiar with. Continuing uphill past the petroglyphs is a high point with a nice view south and north of Comb ridge, and to the west.
