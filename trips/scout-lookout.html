Scout Lookout

12/30/2021 - Scout Lookout

A few days earlier I had hiked up to Scout Lookout (overlooking Angel's Landing) in Zion with Eliot and a few days later after seeing snow in the forecast, I returned by myself to hike past Scout Lookout as far as I could go. Above Scout Lookout there were quite a bit of snow and I hiked 1-2 miles past and the snow got deeper, at least knee deep. One guy coming from the other direction warned that there had been a small avalanche ahead. Feeling a bit cold I turned around and returned to Scout Overlook where I spent a few minutes watching people struggle up the icy Angel's Landing route.
