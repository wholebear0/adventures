North Guardian Angel and West Northgate Peak

8/10/2025 - North Guardian Angel and West Northgate Peak

I had been wanting to do these two peaks for some time. The climb up North Guardian Angel had some exposure in spots and just one or two class 4 sections, but solid handholds. I'd say its more of a Class 3 climb, especially if going on the northern side of the ridge. West Northgate Peaks was a nice walk up and had similar views as North Guardian Angel. It was as nice 5 hour hike.
