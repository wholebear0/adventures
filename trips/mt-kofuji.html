Mt Kofuji

2026-06-06 - Mt Kofuji

In Matsuyama I rented a bicycle for the day from the hotel I was staying downtown by the main train station for $1.50 and biked with no end goal in mind except to reach the coast. Six miles later i found myself cruising along blue water of the coast and eventually stopped at the Takahama train station, staring at the backside of a ferry that left moments before. The island it was headed to was small and not far and I had the bicycle. Why not go check it out? After waiting a few minutes for the ferry to return and paying a few yen for a ticket, I soon found myself aboard on the way to Gogo island, an island 2 miles long and .2 miles wide at its narrowest part. There was a rumble of an engine, a whiff of diesel, a soft maritime breeze, and we're off. The ferry took no longer than 10 minutes and after reaching Tomari port and walking a minute through town, I started up the trail to Mt Kofuji which was steep and muddy but mostly maintained, passing by orange trees. At the top was an abandoned hut and partial but still nice views to the east of the Matsuyama area and to the west of numerous small islands.
