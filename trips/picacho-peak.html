Picacho Peak

11/22/2025 - Picacho Peak

Picacho Peak is in a state park just off interstate 10 and I had driven by maybe 50 times and never stopped. Today I finally made it up and the hike was short and really fun despite the crowd of people. The hike starts and goes up to a saddle point where the off trail peak, Razorback Ridge, is closed to the public. The route then goes over to the other side of the saddle giving views to the west towards Ironwood Forest National Monument. At this point and up to just below the summit there are cable chains to help hikers. There are a few spots where it is really needed like a 30 foot section that is nearly vertical on slick rock. At the summit there was a cluster of people hanging around which seems to be the usual for summits around here that are well known with established trails.
