Grand Wash to Fern's Nipple

4/11/2026 - Grand Wash to Fern's Nipple

This journey through the popular Grand wash to the unpopular but incredible off trail adventure up to Ferns nipple was truly a feast for the eyes and had a little bit of everything. Narrows, ridge, traversing above and around multiple side canyons, a few minor dryfall obstacles, then a slick rock scramble up the peak. And not to mention the awesome 360 degree views. After an early start to the hike before 8am hiking through grand wash from the east trailhead almost to the west trailhead, I turned off the grand wash trail where a cairn marked the beginning of the ascent above the canyon. From that point on there wasnt a single person until joining back up to the grand wash trail on the way down. The route up to Fern's is well marked and cairned though it does involve a bit of route finding and searching for cairns. It was a very windy day with gusts up to 30 or 40 mph so I was worried about the conditions on the summit. After 2 or 3 hours I made it to the base of the peak. The wind had kept up and blue rain clouds were coming in from the north west. I scrambled up to the summit, choosing a line slightly off of center trying to minimize exposure, though there was an unavoidable moment or two of it, and made it to the top. I was up for maybe 10 min because of the questionable skies and extreme winds, but could have spent an hour. The way back down to the wash, zig zagging around side canyons, was quicker than the way up knowing the way.
