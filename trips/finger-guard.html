Finger Guard

2026-02-21 - Finger Guard

This hike follows along the popular Finger Rock trail that goes up to Mt Kimball, but turns off the main trail about 2/3rds the way up onto an unofficial trail which then leads up to a peak overlooking finger rock itself. Starting the hike at about noon the main trail was crowded as expected but there was no one after the turnoff at the saddle. The route descended to a lightly flowing creek then resumed upwards, side hilling up to a saddle a couple hundred feet below the Finger Guard peak. From there it was a fun class 3 scramble among a few hoodoos and interesting rock formations, characteristic of the surrounding Pusch Ridge Wilderness peaks. The views on top were good and it was a nice way to spend a Saturday afternoon.
