Adventures - Palm Canyon

2/15/2026 - Palm Canyon

Turning off highway 95 and continuing for 7 miles will take you on a slightly wash boarded but passable in any vehicle road, past numerous dispersed campers in vehicles and rvs, to the palm canyon trailhead. Here at the end of the road, one can continue on foot for a short one mile roundtrip hike into the canyon to see the only known place in Arizona where palm trees grow naturally. It is a scenic and narrow canyon surrounded by towering cliffs. There aren't too many palms and it's easy to miss them but there is a sign about a third of a mile up pointing to palm trees. They can be found in a side canyon up maybe 100 ft in the distance. I ventured another third of a mile up the canyon hoping to find more palms but the going got to be a scramble over boulders and I ultimately turned back after encountering thick vegetation, though apparently there is a technical route leading up and over to Signal Peak.
