Ashdown Gorge

5/23/2025 - Ashdown Gorge

Absolutely gorgeous gorge with perfect crisp weather. The hike was mostly along and through the creek/river which comes from snowmelt up in the mountains near Brian Head where I like to go skiing. The water was cold when I started the hike around 7:30 am but was refreshing by the back half of the hike. The canyon walls towered over the gorge and were lined densely with ponderosa and fir trees. The temperature was great and the air had the sweet aroma of late spring. I saw only 3 other parties on the hike and really enjoyed this one.
